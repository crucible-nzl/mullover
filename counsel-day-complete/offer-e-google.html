Frequently asked

What does Counsel.day cost?

Your first solo decision is free for life. Additional solo decisions are $9.99 USD each. Couple decisions are $15.99 USD. Family decisions (3-6 participants) are $29.99 USD. No subscription is required.

How long does a Counsel.day decision take?

You choose. The minimum is 7 days; the maximum is 365. Most decisions run for 14 or 30 days. The point is not to decide fast; it is to give the question the time it deserves.

Is this therapy or counselling?

No. Counsel.day is a structured decision tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace therapy or counselling. If you are working through something a therapist would help with, see a therapist.

Can my partner see my votes before the final day?

No. Each evening you cast one private vote. Neither partner can see the other's votes (or notes) until the unseal moment on the final day. The records open at the same minute.

What if we change our mind?

You can extend the duration mid-decision, or close it early. Payments are charged upfront and final at composition; refunds are limited to technical defects on our part. Solo first decisions are free, so trying the product costs nothing.
