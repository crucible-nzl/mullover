Privacy controls

Your data, on your terms.

Counsel.day was built on a sealed read path. Your private votes are sealed in the database until verdict day. Below are the controls that sit underneath that promise.

Export your data Every decision, vote, note, and verdict on your account as a JSON download

⚠ Danger zone

Deleting your account schedules every decision, vote, note, verdict, and synthesis for permanent removal. You have a 14-day grace window in which you can email admin@counsel.day to restore. After 14 days the deletion is final. If you share decisions with a partner, their access ends when the data is removed.

Counsel.day

Two products. Counsel.day Decision is for solo, couples, and families facing a hard joint decision · one private vote each evening on the same question, for a duration you set, sealed until the final day. Counsel Journal is the standalone reflection tool · one minute every evening, a verdict on Monday, a themed verdict every month. Worldwide, priced in USD.
