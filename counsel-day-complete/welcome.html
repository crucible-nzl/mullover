Welcome to Counsel.day

Decide slowly, well.

Counsel.day is not a chat app. It is a sealed-vote tool for decisions you don't want to settle in one evening. Three things to know before you write your first question:

01

One vote per evening.

Each evening at the time you choose, we ask you the same question and store your answer (plus an optional note) in the database. You vote alone. No one else sees your votes until the unseal moment.

02

A duration you set.

7 to 365 days. Most decisions run 14 or 30. The point is not to pick fast; the point is to let your view of the question move over time, and see where it lands.

03

A verdict, on the final evening.

When the duration ends, both records open at the same minute. You see the daily trajectory, the themes that recurred, and an AI synthesis paragraph that observes the record without taking sides.
