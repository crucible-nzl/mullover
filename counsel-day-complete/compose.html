FIELD 01 · THE QUESTION · REQUIRED

What are you deciding?

Phrase it as a single question that can be answered Yes or No. "Should we have a baby?" is better than "Family planning." The question is what you will see every evening of the period.

FIELD 02 · FORMAT · REQUIRED

How do you want to answer each evening?

The four-band Strong/Lean format is the default and what the synthesis on the final day is tuned for. The simpler binary Yes/No is available for questions where the strength of the answer is not the interesting axis.

RANKED OPTIONS · 3 TO 6 CHOICES

List the choices you want to rank each evening. Examples · five cities you're considering · four job offers · three childcare options · two universities and an apprenticeship. The verdict on the close date names which option kept landing at the top across the period and which slid down over time.

FIELD 03 · DURATION · REQUIRED

How long should the period run?

Match the duration to the weight of the question. A week suits a house with an offer deadline. A month suits most career and home decisions. Sixty days suits partnership questions. Ninety days suits reproductive and life-shape questions. Annual is reserved for the largest decisions only.

FIELD 04 · INVITED PARTNER · OPTIONAL

Running this with someone?

Add a partner by email to run the decision jointly. Either of you may invite, and the invitation is single-use. Leave blank to run a Solo decision; you may add a partner later, but only before any votes have been cast.

Quick-pick from saved contacts

People you invite are saved automatically · you will not need to retype their email next time.
