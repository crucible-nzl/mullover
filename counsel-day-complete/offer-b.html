Counsel.day

A new mechanism for hard decisions

Decide slowly, well.

Sealed votes. A duration you choose. One verdict, opened together on the final day.

How it works

01
Write the question. Pick a duration. 7 to 365 days.
02
Each evening, vote privately. Optionally write a line · or speak it (30s voice-to-text on paid tiers). Sealed in the database.
03
On the final day, both records open at the same minute. The verdict appears.

A five-layer analysis · on the final day

SynthesisA 400-600 word paragraph reading the record back to you.
TrajectoryThe day-by-day shape of every vote, both partners.
ThemesThe words and topics that recurred most.
AsymmetryWhere your vocabulary and convictions diverged.
ConversationOne specific question to ask after reading.
