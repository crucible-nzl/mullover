Products and pricing

Edit the display name, description, price, and Stripe Price ID for each public tier. Changes are audit-logged and shown on /pricing within five minutes of save.

Pricing flow: Stripe Price objects are immutable. To change a price, create a new Price in Stripe Dashboard (Products → counsel-day-prod), copy its price_xxx id, and paste it into Stripe Price ID below alongside the new display value. The amount you charge is whatever the linked Stripe Price says, regardless of what's typed here. This screen controls only the customer-facing display.
