THE REVEAL

On the final evening both verdicts publish together.

The reveal happens at one instant for everyone. The written verdict paragraphs publish on the same page. Underneath them: the agreement rate across the period, the conviction trajectory for each of you, the themes clustered from your notes, the synthesis paragraph naming what was actually being disagreed about underneath the surface question, and one specific concrete question to open the conversation that follows.

Nothing about the analysis is reachable before the close date. The keys are bound to the date; the database row is sealed-read; not even a member of staff can look. Every participant sees the verdict at the same instant, no one anchoring anyone else beforehand.
