WHAT WE DO NOT DO

No ticket queue, no AI, no canned response.

We do not run a support helpdesk. No ticket number is generated. No chatbot replies for us. No auto-responder is set up except the read-receipt your provider may send. The team is small enough that every message is read by a person, and the person who reads it is the one who replies.

We cannot intervene in the data of a sealed decision. The architecture genuinely will not let us see it. If your question is about a decision currently running, the most useful thing we can do is answer questions about the format, the duration, the privacy mechanism, the analysis, or the billing. We cannot show you a partner's vote, cancel the seal, or modify the verdict.

For genuinely distressing situations, our reply will include the relevant national helpline alongside our own response. Counsel.day is not an emergency service; if you need immediate help, please reach a crisis line in your country before you write to us.
