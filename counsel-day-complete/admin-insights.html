Insights

Activation funnel, vote-time heatmap, and cohort retention. All three computed live from the database; no caching. Use this page to spot where the funnel leaks, when to schedule the evening-prompt cron, and whether new cohorts are returning.

Activation funnel

Six stages from sign-up to a second decision. The bar widths are absolute counts; the percentages between rows are stage-to-stage conversion.

Vote-time heatmap

Votes cast in the last 90 days, bucketed by day-of-week and hour-of-day (UTC). Darker means more votes in that bucket. Use it to tune the evening-prompt cron schedule.

Cohort retention

Users grouped by signup month. Each row shows how many of that cohort voted in each subsequent month. Colour intensity is % of the cohort still active.
