The Tuesday-Thursday problem.

On the schooling decision James and Alexandra could not settle at the dinner table, the answer was obviously yes on Tuesday and obviously no on Thursday. The difference between the two evenings was not new information; the difference was them. This essay is the long version of how that observation, sat with for long enough, became the founding insight of Counsel.day: a decision worth losing sleep over deserves a season, not a sitting, and the analysis on the final day should read the arc rather than picking the latest point.
