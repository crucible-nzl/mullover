Cron controls

Trigger any background job immediately. Output is captured below and audit-logged.

evening-prompt
Email "tonight's vote" prompts to active-decision participants who have not voted today.
verdict-generate
For every decision past unseals_at, generate verdict via Anthropic + email participants.
session-purge
DELETE FROM sessions WHERE expires_at < NOW().
invite-expiry
NULL invite_tokens older than 30 days; cancel parent decision.
invite-reminder
Single reminder email 48h after invite (tracked in audit_log).
hard-delete-purge
Permanently delete users soft-deleted > 14 days ago + cascade.
audit-prune
Trim audit_log: 24mo retention general · 7y retention for refunds & hard-deletes.
pg-dump
Postgres backup to /home/deploy/backups/ (gzipped, in-process tsx).
sitemap
Crawl /var/www and regenerate sitemap.xml (in-process tsx).
weekly-digest
Sunday-evening ops summary · emails OPS_DIGEST_EMAIL with 7-day signups, verdicts, AI spend, push, refunds, cron failures.
security-audit
Daily npm audit + classify findings. Critical + high trigger an email to OPS_DIGEST_EMAIL with paste-ready fix instructions. View dashboard.
journal-digest
Sunday-evening · for every Daily Counsel user with 3+ unsealed entries in the past week, generates and emails a Monday-morning verdict (positives, strains, throughline, question).
