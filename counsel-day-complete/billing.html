How billing works

Counsel.day uses Stripe for payments. Every charge is made directly by Stripe and shows up under "Counsel.day" on your bank statement. Invoices and receipts are emailed to the address on your account at the moment of each charge.

The Stripe Customer Portal lets you update your card, change your billing address, and cancel or pause your subscription. Refunds follow the policy on refunds.html · we refund only where our tool failed or where local consumer-protection law requires it. No change-of-mind, no partial-completion refunds.
