Pain 01

You keep deciding differently.

Yes on Tuesday after a hard day at work. No on Sunday in the garden. Neither answer is a lie; both are partial. A single conversation captures one mood, not the question.

Pain 02

One of you keeps anchoring the other.

Whoever speaks first sets the room. Whoever speaks loudest moves the mood. The result is a synthesis of two people performing reasonableness, not what either of you actually thinks.

Pain 03

The question keeps coming back.

The decision gets postponed, or settled on a misleading data point, then circles back two weeks later, tired and worse. The indecision is rarely a shortage of information.

Step 01 · 30 seconds

Set the question and the duration.

Choose one question, pick the duration the decision actually deserves · a week, a month, ninety days · and we hold you to it. You may extend; you may not shorten.

Step 02 · One evening at a time

Vote privately, every evening, for the duration.

One tap on a 5-point scale, plus an optional one-line note (type it, or tap the mic on paid tiers for 30s of voice-to-text). Your partner never sees your vote or your note. They only know whether you voted that day.

Step 03 · Verdict morning

Both verdicts unlock together.

On the final day, at the same instant for both of you, the analysis opens: agreement rate, conviction trajectory, themes, synthesis, and one specific next conversation.

File · What makes Counsel.day different

Four properties no other tool in the category has at the same time.

Property 01

The private vote.

Your votes and notes are sealed in the database until the final day. No mid-period peek, no admin override, no support escape hatch. The privacy is the mechanism, not a setting.

Property 02

Sealed until day N.

You set the duration once, at the start. The verdict cannot be opened early, by either of you, for any reason. A decision worth losing sleep over deserves the whole arc.

Property 03

Five-layer analysis.

Agreement rate, conviction trajectory, theme extraction, synthesis, conversation prompt. Not a score. A map of where you agreed, where you split, and the axis underneath.

Property 04

Worldwide, priced in USD.

Counsel.day works in any country with internet. Pricing is the same everywhere · $9.99 USD per Solo decision, $15.99 USD per Couple, $29.99 USD per Family. First Solo decision is free.
