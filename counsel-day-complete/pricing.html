WHEN CONSUMER ANNUAL MAKES SENSE

Start per-decision. Most people stay there.

The per-decision prices are the product's default and they are fair on their own. A meaningful decision is not a subscription; treating it like one would push the product toward urgency it does not deserve. We never push you to upgrade and we will not bundle annual into the checkout flow as a default.

Consumer Annual pays for itself if any of these describe your year:

  • Four or more Couple decisions ($25 USD each · $100 USD already exceeds the $99 USD annual)
  • Two or more Family decisions ($49 USD each · $98 USD nearly covers it)
  • A mixed year of Solo, Couple, and Family decisions where the total would otherwise exceed about $100 USD

If you are unsure, start per-decision. You can switch to Consumer Annual at any time; the per-decision amounts paid in the past 30 days are credited against the annual price.

PAYMENT, REFUNDS, TAXES

The fine print, in plain English.

Currency. All prices on this page are in US dollars, charged to your card via Stripe from anywhere in the world. We use Stripe's worldwide payment surface; you'll see the conversion to your local currency on your statement.

Taxes. Counsel.day is not GST-registered today, so no GST or sales tax is added at checkout. The price shown is the price billed. When we cross the GST registration threshold we will add tax collection and you will see it as a separate line on the receipt.

When you are charged. Upfront, on the day you compose a decision or the day you start Consumer Annual. We do not authorise-and-hold across the long duration of a decision (a Stripe authorisation can be held for at most 7 days, and our decisions can run for up to 365), so every charge is a real charge taken at composition.

Refunds. Charges are final at checkout. We refund only for a closed list of technical defects on our part (verdict pipeline failed, double-charge, decision cut short, product offline for a meaningful portion of your decision) and where local consumer-protection law mandates a refund we cannot contract out of. No change-of-mind, no partial-completion, no didn't-use-it refunds. Full policy on refunds.

Receipts and invoices. Every charge is followed by a Stripe receipt to the email on your account. PDF invoices are available on the billing page in your account; export the full history as CSV any time.

Cancellation. Cancel Consumer Annual from your account at any time; the plan covers the remainder of the current term then ends without renewing. The annual fee already paid is not refundable; cancellation prevents the next renewal only.

QUESTIONS ABOUT PRICING

The questions people actually ask.

Is the free Solo decision really free?

Yes. No card on file, no trial period that auto-upgrades. Your first lifetime Solo decision is free and ships with a Python numerical summary on the reveal day. The AI-written synthesis paragraph is reserved for paid decisions because that's where the per-decision cost on our side lives; the rest of the product is the same on free and paid Solo.

Why is Couple more than Solo · do I pay for my partner?

Your partner does not pay separately. The Couple price ($25 USD per decision) covers both of you, plus the additional analysis cost on our side: two streams of votes, two sets of notes, the axis-of-disagreement synthesis, and the per-participant breakdown. A Couple verdict is materially more expensive for us to generate than a Solo verdict.

What is Consumer Annual, exactly?

One SKU at $99 USD per year. While active, any consumer decision you start on the account is included at no additional charge · Solo, Couple, or Family. There is no per-decision-type sub-plan; the annual covers all three. Designed for the rare household whose year genuinely calls for several decisions; not a default upsell.

What happens to a decision that is mid-run if my Consumer Annual ends?

It runs to completion on the original duration. We honour every decision that was started under an active plan; cancellation does not interrupt or close out any in-flight decision. Only newly composed decisions after the plan ends would require a per-decision charge or a fresh annual.

Are there discounts for therapists or counsellors?

Therapists and counsellors who refer clients to Counsel.day receive a quarterly referral payout for client accounts that sign up through their referral link. See the therapist program or counsellor program page for the detail. There is no separate practitioner plan today; the Consumer Annual covers all client decisions you run on a single account.
