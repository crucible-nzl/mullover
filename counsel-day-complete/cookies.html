ANALYTICS

Google Analytics 4, by default.

Counsel.day uses Google Analytics 4 to count pageviews, measure broad referral patterns, and detect outages from the user-side. GA4 is off by default and fires only after you accept it in the consent banner. Aggregate counts are useful; individual journeys are not. When enabled we set GA4 with IP anonymisation, a fourteen-month retention, no Google Signals, and no advertising features.

You can change your mind any time:

  • Decline analytics in the consent banner on first visit; the banner will not re-appear, and your choice is stored in cd_consent_v1 in your browser's localStorage.
  • Send a Global Privacy Control or Do-Not-Track header. We honour both silently; the banner does not appear and GA4 does not load.
  • Use the buttons under 05 below to grant or revoke at any time without re-opening the banner.
  • Install Google's official browser opt-out add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

WHAT WE DO NOT SET

Categories that are absent.

We do not set marketing cookies, advertising pixels, retargeting tags, social-network share-trackers, or session-replay tools. Counsel.day does not use Meta Pixel, X Pixel, LinkedIn Insight, Reddit Pixel, Pinterest Tag, TikTok Pixel, Hotjar, FullStory, Heap, or Mixpanel. We have no plan to add them. If that changes, we will publish the change here at least thirty days before the cookie first fires.

No marketing pixels. No retargeting. No session replay. If you see one, it is a bug; please write to us.
MANAGING YOUR PREFERENCES

How to change your mind.

Use the buttons below to grant or revoke analytics consent immediately. Your current status will update on the next page load.

  • Clear site data in your browser to reset every Counsel.day cookie and the cd_consent_v1 localStorage entry; the consent banner will then re-appear on your next visit.
  • Block third-party cookies in your browser settings; the product still runs because all essential cookies are first-party.
  • Email privacy@counsel.day with any cookie question we have not answered above; we respond within five business days.
