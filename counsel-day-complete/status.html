Counsel.day

A decision tool for solo, couples, and families, for the questions a single conversation cannot resolve. Each evening, you cast one private vote on the same question, for a duration you set, with every vote sealed in the database until the final day. When the verdict opens, you see where you converged, where you diverged, the themes underneath your own words, and one specific next conversation. The deciding stays with you; the structure is ours. Worldwide, priced in USD.
